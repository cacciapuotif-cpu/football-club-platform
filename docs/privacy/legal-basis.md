# Legal Bases Summary

| Data Category | Purpose | Legal Basis |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Wellness surveys & psychometrics | Monitor wellbeing, provide support | Art. 6(1)(a) consent + Art. 9(2)(a) explicit consent |
| Session load/GPS | Manage workload, optimize training | Art. 6(1)(f) legitimate interest (balanced) + Art. 9(2)(h) medical purposes |
| Injury & medical logs | Treatment, RTP decisions | Art. 6(1)(d) vital interests / Art. 9(2)(h) medical care |
| Video analytics | Tactical review, compliance | Art. 6(1)(a) consent |
| Reports & exports | Operational decisions | Depends on source data; adhere to retention/pseudonymization policies |
