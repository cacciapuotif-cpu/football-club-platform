# Data Processing Agreement (DPA) Checklist

- Controller & Processor identified with legal names and contact points.
- Data categories enumerated (wellness, sessions, medical, video).
- Purpose and lawful basis documented.
- Processing instructions clearly defined; no sub-processing without consent.
- Subprocessors listed with geography & safeguards.
- Security measures described (RBAC, pseudonymization, encryption).
- Data subject rights handling and SLAs specified.
- Retention & deletion clauses referencing `/docs/privacy/retention-policy.md`.
